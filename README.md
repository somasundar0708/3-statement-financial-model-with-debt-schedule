Retail Business Financial Model (Excel)

Overview

A dynamic 3-statement financial model built in Excel for a multi-channel retail business (Big Box + Online revenue streams). The model projects performance across a 5-year period (2026–2030) with monthly granularity for the forecast period, and includes full debt, working capital, and covenant compliance tracking — built to lender/credit-analysis standards.

Key Features


3-Statement Integration: Fully linked Income Statement, Balance Sheet, and Statement of Cash Flows
Revenue Modeling: Separate growth assumptions for Big Box (1.5% YoY) and Online (20% YoY) revenue streams
Debt Schedule: Revolver + Term Debt with automated cash flow sweep mechanism
Working Capital Schedule: Driven by DSO, DOH (Days on Hand), and DPO assumptions
Capex & Depreciation Schedule: Straight-line depreciation with a rolling asset base, including a modeled capital expansion project
Bonus & Tax Accrual Schedules: Month-by-month accrual and cash payment timing
Covenant Compliance Tracking:

Leverage Ratio (Total Debt / EBITDA) — covenant threshold 2.5x, tightening to 2.0x
Fixed Charge Coverage Ratio (FCCR) — covenant threshold 1.25x



Error Checking: Master error-check panel validating balance sheet balance, cash flow tie-outs, and schedule consistency (zero-error output)
